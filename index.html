Instructions:
Keep the tab open (do not minimise!)
Specify a location(s) and a driving time in minutes.
Multiple locations can be entered with the separator "//" i.e. two slashes
Wait for the plotting to finish (green marker will appear in the centre) before adjusting
Points coloured in green are a 'good-match'; whereas those in red are too 'high' and in blue too 'low'.
Manually adjust the points as desired to achieve a more accurate isochrone.
Please note that in this version that amount of rays is limited to 8 (to save bandwidth) and travel-time is traffic free.
